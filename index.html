campus life

school library

Was established in 2019

Our school has a ventilated, computerized and centralized Air-Conditioned library for the students. This library is well stocked with a wide collection of books. This range of books consists of Textbooks, Reference books, Fiction books (English, Hindi), Encylopedias, Magazines and much more.

school language lab

Was established in 2019

In this Language Lab, we want our students to learn International Languages. We have study materials for students, which helps them to communicate fluently in a choosen language as fast as possible. Further, the specialized books also offer insight into the cultures in which those languages are spoken.

school computer lab

Was established in 2019

Today technology has affected every sphere of life, especially eduction. This has changed our perception of what a school should provides to its students. In this time of booming technology, the commputer labs are appropriate platforms for the students to nurture their future.
